My Methodology.
01
Discover
I start to discover about users & business needs, brainstorim & finding out whether the product is a good fit.
02
Research
I findout target audience, user & business goals, & research on market about the problem that we are trying to solve.
03
Sketch
I brainstorm all ideas on paper and create fast wireframes. Later using different tools, I convert them to hi-fidelity designs.
04
Prototype
I take my hi-fidelity designs to prototyping mode using various tools and make it interactive so it can be tested.
05
Test
I get feedback from clients & users about my design & prototype. I iterate my design based on the feedback.
06
Deploy
Once the prototype is validated, I take my designs to dev mode. I code them using HTML, CSS & JS and deploy them.
